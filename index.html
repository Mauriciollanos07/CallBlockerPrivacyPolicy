1. Information We Collect

CallBlocker collects and processes the following information to provide call blocking functionality:

  • Phone Numbers: Incoming call numbers to determine blocking actions
  • Call Metadata: Call timestamps and blocking decisions for app functionality
  • Contact Information: Access to your contacts to identify trusted callers
  • Phone State: Detection of incoming calls to apply blocking rules

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Blocking unwanted calls based on your preferences
  • Allowing trusted contacts to reach you
  • Maintaining a local history of blocked calls
  • Providing call screening and silencing functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload your data to external servers
  • No Sharing: Your call data is never shared with third parties
  • Encryption: Data is protected using Android's built-in security features

4. Permissions Explained

  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Required to detect incoming calls
  • READ_CONTACTS: Used to identify trusted contacts
  • CALL_PHONE: Enables call screening functionality

5. Data Retention

Call blocking data is retained locally until you:

  • Manually delete entries from the app
  • Uninstall the application
  • Clear the app's data through device settings

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View all blocked call data within the app
  • Delete individual call records
  • Revoke app permissions at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

7. Third-Party Services

CallBlocker does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
